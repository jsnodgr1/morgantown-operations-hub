# North Plant Scope Definition

This project is strictly limited to **Morgantown North Plant operations**.

---

## Included Areas
- Liquids (K-units)
- Blends (P1, P2, A-Blends)
- Solids / K9 / 195
- Shipping & logistics impacting North Plant

---

## Exclusions
- South Plant documentation
- Archived schedules unless explicitly required
- Corporate or site-wide policy emails
- Historical planning artifacts outside the report window
